Quick answer

The top pick for a budget electric shaver is the Philips Norelco OneBlade 360 because it delivers a surprisingly close, comfortable shave without breaking the bank, and its versatile trimmer handles both stubble and longer hair.

If you want the closest foil shave under a few hundred dollars, go with the Braun Series 5; if portability is your priority, the Panasonic ES-LL20 is the best compact choice for travel and quick touch-ups.

Shopping for a budget electric shaver doesn’t mean you have to settle for a rough, tugging shave. The best affordable models now pack features like sharp self-sharpening blades, flexible heads, and even wet/dry operation – all without crossing into luxury pricing. The key is knowing what trade-offs you can live with. To pick the right one, look at your hair type and shaving habits. Coarse, dense beards benefit from foil shavers with higher cutter counts; sensitive skin often does better with rotary heads or hypoallergenic foils. Also consider whether you want a corded or cordless model, how easy it is to clean, and if you’ll use it for edging or full-face grooming. Best for specific needs

Best for sensitive skin

If your skin gets red and irritated after shaving, look for a model with a flexible head that minimizes pressure points. The Philips Norelco OneBlade 360 is a great budget-friendly pick for sensitive skin because its dual-sided blade cuts hair without pulling the skin, and you can use it dry without getting razor burn. Many users report less irritation than with traditional foil shavers. Our pick: Philips Norelco OneBlade 360.

Best for travel and commuting

When you need a shaver that disappears into a gym bag or carry-on, compact size and battery convenience matter most. The Panasonic ES-LL20 is the clear winner: it runs on a single AA battery so you never hunt for a charger, and its small footprint means you can forget it’s there until you need it. It handles a few days of stubble well and costs very little. Our pick: Panasonic ES-LL20.

Best for a close foil shave under a budget-friendly price

Not everyone wants to spend top dollar, but still craves that baby-smooth feel. The Braun Series 5 gives you the closest result of any shaver in this roundup, thanks to its adaptive foil system and powerful motor. It’s perfect for business professionals or anyone who wants a daily shave that looks freshly done without the premium price tag. Our pick: Braun Series 5.

Our verdict

At the end of the day, your best budget electric shaver comes down to what you value most: the versatile, skin-friendly performance of the Philips Norelco OneBlade 360, the close foil shave of the Braun Series 5, or the ultra-portable Panasonic ES-LL20. All three deliver far more than their price suggests, so choose the one that fits your beard, your skin, and your daily routine. You won’t be disappointed.
