Electric shavers have come a long way from the nickel-and-scraping days. Today&#8217;s models use multiple cutting elements, flexible heads, and clever coatings to deliver a shave that can rival a blade &#8212; without the irritation. Wet/dry capability is nice if you like shaving gel, but a dry foil shaver often provides the closest cut.
